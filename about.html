About me

Hi, I'm Cole

What's up!

I’m a UX/UI designer with a writing background. Through extensive work with disruptive startups and entrepreneurs, I realized that I have a love and affinity for both problem-seeking and solution-creating. Putting myself in the user’s shoes is something that comes naturally to me, and I use this skill to create high impact, human-centered designs. As a lifelong learner, I’m looking forward to working in a fast-paced environment in climate-tech. My passions are helping people, greener design, moving towards a circular economy, and envisioning a better future.

Skills

What I do when I'm not working

I have a profound love for the Earth and I try to get outside to enjoy her every day. Whether it's hiking, yoga, or laying in the sun, you'll find me outside anytime I'm not at my desk. I also love meditating. There is something so special about seeing the world with a calm mind. Beautiful places like Arches National Park (picture to the right) help me have a clear mind. With this clear mind, I hope to create things that can make the world a more fun, clean, and happy place to live in.
